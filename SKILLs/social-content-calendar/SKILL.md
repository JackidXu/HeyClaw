# Social Media Content Calendar & Generation

Generate a complete 30-day content calendar with platform-specific posts, hooks, hashtags, and a content repurposing strategy.

## Phase 1: Brand and Audience Discovery

Establish before generating any content:

- Brand name, Industry, Target audience, Brand voice
- Key products/services, Unique selling points, Competitors

### Platform Selection

| Platform      | Best For                         | Posting Frequency |
| ------------- | -------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| **LinkedIn**  | B2B, SaaS, professionals         | 3-5x/week         |
| **Twitter/X** | Tech, media, real-time           | 1-3x/day          |
| **Instagram** | E-commerce, lifestyle, creators  | 4-7x/week         |
| **TikTok**    | Consumer brands, education       | 1-3x/day          |
| **YouTube**   | Education, SaaS demos, long-form | 1-2x/week         |
| **Facebook**  | Local business, communities      | 3-5x/week         |

Select 2-3 primary platforms.

## Phase 2: Content Strategy Framework

### Content Pillars & Mix

| Pillar   | Type              | Mix |
| -------- | ----------------- | --- |
| Pillar 1 | Educational       | 40% |
| Pillar 2 | Behind-the-Scenes | 20% |
| Pillar 3 | Social Proof      | 15% |
| Pillar 4 | Engagement        | 15% |
| Pillar 5 | Promotional       | 10% |

## Phase 3: Hook Formulas (Platform-Specific)

**LinkedIn Hooks:**

- "I [did/learned/lost/gained] [specific thing] and here's what happened:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "[Number] years in [industry]. Here's what nobody tells you:"
- "Stop [common practice]. Start [better alternative]."

**Twitter/X Hooks:**

- "Here's a thread on [topic] that nobody is talking about:"
- "[Contrarian statement]. Let me explain."
- "Hot take: [bold claim]"

**Instagram Hooks:**

- "Save this for later" (educational)
- "I tested [X] for [time]. Results inside."
- "The [topic] nobody talks about:"

**TikTok Hooks (First 3 Seconds):**

- "Wait, you're still doing [old way]?"
- "Here's the [topic] hack nobody showed you"
- "If you're a [audience], watch this"

## Phase 4: Hashtag Strategy

Tiered approach:

- **Niche** (Under 100K posts): 3-5 hashtags
- **Medium** (100K-1M posts): 3-5 hashtags
- **Broad** (1M+ posts): 2-3 hashtags
- **Branded** (Custom): 1 hashtag

Platform-specific counts: Instagram 5-15, LinkedIn 3-5, Twitter 1-2, TikTok 3-5

## Phase 5: Engagement Tactics

- **Questions:** End 30% of posts with open-ended questions
- **Polls:** Use 1-2x per week
- **Controversial/Debate Posts:** 1-2x per week
- **Storytelling Posts:** 1-2x per week

## Phase 6: Content Repurposing (1-to-10 Framework)

Take ONE long-form piece → 10+ social posts:

1. LinkedIn text post - Key insight
2. Twitter thread - 5-7 takeaways
3. Instagram carousel - Main framework
4. Instagram Reel - 30-second summary
5. TikTok - Quick tip format
6. LinkedIn carousel - PDF slideshow
7. Twitter single tweet - Most quotable line
8. Instagram Story - Behind-the-scenes
9. YouTube Short - Condensed version
10. Facebook post - Discussion question

## Phase 7: 30-Day Calendar Structure

Generate day-by-day content with:

- Platform, Content Pillar, Hook, Full Post Text
- Hashtags, Posting Time, Content Type
- Visual Description (for image/video platforms)

## Output Format

Save to `SOCIAL-CALENDAR.md` with:

- Brand Context summary
- Content Pillars breakdown
- Hashtag Strategy
- Week-by-week calendar (4 weeks)
- Repurposing Strategy
- Engagement Playbook
- Metrics to Track
