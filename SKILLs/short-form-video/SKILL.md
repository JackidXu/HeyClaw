# Short-Form Video Production

Guide short-form video creation from concept through publication using practitioner methodology: fast prototyping, finding what works before building process, and "sponge then sharpen" creative philosophy.

**Core Philosophy:** You can't build a franchise system if you're not already selling a billion cheeseburgers. Find what works FIRST, then build process around it.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating original short-form video content (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
- Cutting podcast clips for short-form distribution
- Developing video formats and testing new concepts
- Optimizing existing video content for better performance

---

## The Sponge-Then-Sharpen Method

### Phase 1: Sponge (Hunt for What Works)

- Prototype fast (15 minutes max per video)
- Publish everything - "I don't have bad clips"
- Test multiple formats, hooks, styles
- Don't build systems yet
- Accept chaos as normal for creative work

### Phase 2: Sharpen (Triple Down on Winners)

- Build process around proven format
- Optimize the working format
- Create templates and systems
- Delegate production

**When to shift:** You've found a format that consistently gets views AND you could make it repeatedly.

---

## The 15-Minute Rule

**If you're working on short-form content for longer than 15 minutes, that's a warning sign.** Get it 90% good. The last 10% would take 2.5 hours - not worth it.

## Hook Construction

### The McDonald's Test

> If a truck driver wouldn't understand your hook, it's too complicated.

### The 3-Second Window

The first 1-3 seconds determine everything.

### 7 Hook Categories That Work

1. **Polarizing Statements** - Challenge common assumptions
2. **Counter-Intuitive Reveals** - Flip expected wisdom
3. **Direct Challenges** - Speak directly to specific audience
4. **Curiosity Gaps** - Incomplete information demanding resolution
5. **List Format** - Promise structure and takeaways
6. **观点冲突型** - 挑战认知的观点
7. **数据震撼型** - 用数据打破认知

### Hook Optimization

- **Punchline First:** Put the payoff at the beginning
- **Same Video, Different Hooks:** Test different hooks, spaced over weeks

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### YouTube Shorts

- Include #Shorts tag
- Thumbnail + title still matter for browse/search

### Instagram Reels

- 60 seconds acceptable (shorter usually better)
- Reels get 2-3x reach vs static posts

### TikTok

- Can publish 4-5 times daily
- "I don't have bad clips, I publish everything" mindset

## Title & Thumbnail (YouTube)

### Click-Through Rate Targets

- Below 4%: Low (fix title/thumbnail)
- 4-6%: Ideal range
- Above 6%: Excellent

### Thumbnail Principles

- **Dial It to 11:** Most extreme version
- **Stock Photos > Illustrations**
- **Less Text, Higher Contrast**
- **Show Expressive Faces**

### Title Principles

- **Every Word After Hook Is a Filter:** Broader = more clicks
- **Universalize When Possible**
- **Direct Appeal Format:** "If your kid hates school, please watch this"

## Algorithm Optimization

### Triple Word Score

Stack four signals - algorithm indexes ALL:

1. **Audio transcript** (what's spoken)
2. **On-screen text** (captions, titles)
3. **Caption/description** (text below)
4. **Hashtags** (still matter)

### First 10 Seconds

Topic words must appear in audio, on-screen text, and visual context.

## Production Checklist

### Before Creating

- [ ] What format? (Tier 1/2/3)
- [ ] Hook passes McDonald's Test?
- [ ] All assets ready?
- [ ] Time limit: 15 minutes max

### Before Publishing

- [ ] Triple Word Score complete
- [ ] Platform requirements met
- [ ] Title/thumbnail complementary (YouTube)
- [ ] Hashtags appropriate

### After Publishing

- [ ] Track performance
- [ ] If low views: revise title/thumbnail
- [ ] If it works: plan to make more
- [ ] Don't delete - old content can resurfaces

---

## Common Mistakes

**Production:** Working too long, over-polishing before proving format, building systems before finding winners

**Hook:** Burying the lede, fancy vocabulary, giving away payoff completely, clickbait

**Algorithm:** Topic words missing in first 10 seconds, inconsistent signals, same copy across platforms

---

## Content Pillars Strategy

Build multiple independent pillars - each with its own methodology. When one works, triple down.
